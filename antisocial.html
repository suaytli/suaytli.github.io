“Antisocial Media”

Anti-social media is a movement defined by many intellectual spheres. These spheres uses the lenses of corporate analysis, psychology, data protectionism, sociology, and more... But what I focus with the anti-social media movement of mine is that I always yearned for the MySpace style customizable web-pages. I found the idea of developing stylish and personal artifacts super cool because of my artsy childhood. So this is my chance to try my best to keep an irregular blog here. I am not that type of person to post Instagram or LinkedIn content daily so this is a way for me to produce "output". You can check here for a good overview of anti-social media notion.
